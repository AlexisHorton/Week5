The Avengers introduce the idea of the "Multi-Verse"

The Multiverse is the aggregate of all dimensions and parallel realities in existence. Although dimensions are just portions of reality within a universe, there are also parallel universes that coexist with one another. The Multiverse is believed to be explained by the eternal inflation system, however, the exact nature of its existence is largely unknown to its inhabitants.
